# Legal disclaimer / Aviso legal

## English

**arras-ai is not a lawyer and does not provide legal advice.**

This software is an experimental tool that uses a large language model (LLM) to
extract and classify information from Spanish earnest-money contracts (*contratos
de arras*). Its output is an automated, best-effort interpretation of a document
and is provided **"as is", for informational and educational purposes only**.

By using this software you acknowledge and accept that:

- **It is not a substitute for professional legal advice.** Nothing produced by
  this tool constitutes legal advice, a legal opinion, or the practice of law.
  Before signing, contesting, or acting on any *contrato de arras*, consult a
  qualified lawyer (*abogado*) or notary (*notario*) licensed in Spain.
- **It can be wrong.** LLMs make mistakes. The tool may misclassify the type of
  arras, miss clauses, hallucinate details that are not in the document, or
  misread amounts and dates. **Always verify every result against the original
  contract.**
- **No lawyer–client relationship is created** by downloading, running, or
  relying on this software.
- **The authors and contributors accept no liability** for any loss or damage
  arising from the use of, or reliance on, this software or its output, to the
  fullest extent permitted by law. See the [MIT License](LICENSE).
- **The type of arras has real financial consequences.** In Spain, an
  ambiguously drafted contract is interpreted by default as *arras
  confirmatorias* (the most binding modality). Getting this wrong can cost the
  deposit — or more. This is precisely why you should involve a professional.

If you do not agree with this disclaimer, do not use the software.
